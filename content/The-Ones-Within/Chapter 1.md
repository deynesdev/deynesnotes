The warm autumn morning light woke me around seven. Rubbing my dry, sleepy eyes, I got out of bed and went to the bathroom, where Louis was already.

"Good morning, buddy. Found your hamster?" I said, taking toothpaste and putting it on my brush.

"No, nowhere to be found..." he said sadly.

"Maybe he… you know?" With the toothbrush in my mouth, I nodded toward the toilet. If you looked closely at the water, you could see a little tuft of fur floating in it.

Louis looked at me like I was a wild animal. "What are you even saying! He would never do something like that!"

I silently brushed my teeth and left the bathroom. I needed to text Jacob and ask what he had planned for Leyley. It would be really awkward if I bought her the same thing as him. Equally awkward if I got something she already had, so I decided I’d also write to her best friend, Roxy, once I figured out what I wanted to give.

"So, where we headin'?'

I looked at Jacob.

"I thought you wanted to grab some pizza?"

"Oh yeah!"

"Pizza it is then"

We went to the pizzeria that was the nearest to our university. Pizza in there is not the best in the city, but at least it's cheap.

When we got to the place and sat at our tables, Jacob looked at me with a serious gaze.

"So, you decided in the present for Ashley? The birthday is tomorrow, you know? this time for real". He smirked, laughing at his own joke.

"That was **not** funny, man". I smiled too. "Y'know, I was thinking about it for the whole day, but I just don't know what to do. I have too many ideas and I simply don't know which one to choose"

Jacob leaned back in his chair. "You can always just give her money".

"Nah man. That's.. No. Just no". I leaned back too.

"Okay"

We looked at each other like that for a solid 30 seconds or so. 

"Are you ready to order?", said the waitress, who appeared, seemingly out of nowhere, to my left.

We both leaned forward and looked at the menu.

"Uhh yeah... A big pizza with salami, please", I said.

"And 2 beers", Jacob added.

"And a cider for me then"

"Okay the wait is approximately 20 minutes"

"Sure, thanks", I answered, and the waitress disappeared.

I looked at Jacob again.

"Okay, I think I want to do something myself. Maybe than she'll fall in love with me or something"

Jacob couldn't contain his laugh.

"B-ha-ha-ha-ha!!" 

He then got serious again. "Okay what are the ideas?"

We've been brainstorming ideas for a DIY present for around 20 minutes when that same waitress interrupted us.

"Here are your drinks..", she said while placing 2 big glasses of beer in front of Jacob and my cider before me.

She smiled at us and said, "I'll be back with the pizza in a second", and walked back to the kitchen.

"Bro I didn't think these glasses would be THAT huge!"

They really were big, each one containing around half a liter.

We then smelled something very tasty and both turned our heads to the side.

"And here's the pizza. Enjoy your meal!", the waitress placed the big, steaming pizza with slices of ham and pineapple right in front of us and walked away.

"Wait, didn't I order the one with Salami?"

"Huh? We always get the pineapple one here, man", Jacob stated and grabbed the biggest slice of pizza with his right hand.

I stared at him. *Did we?* I could have sworn... I opened my mouth, then closed it.

"Whatever, I like pineapple pizza too", I muttered, pulling a slice.

We ate the pizza and drank our drinks while chatting about university stuff. Walking out I felt how full my stomach was.

"That was a great meal. Hey, I was thinking about going to the "DIY" store to grab some props for Ashley's present. Wanna go with me?"

"Ah. crap, I can't", Jacob said, pulling out his phone. His screen lit up with a text. "Coach just called a last-minute training session, I gotta run, man"

"Sure, brother. Take care"

We did our usual slap-and-slide handshake and went our ways.
